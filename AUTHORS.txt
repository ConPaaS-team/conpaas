ConPaaS (http://www.conpaas.eu) is developed by Vrije Universiteit
Amsterdam, Zuse Institut Berlin, and XLAB in the Contrail European
research project (http://contrail-project.eu/). Contrail is partially
funded by the FP7 Programme of the European Commission under Grant
Agreement FP7-ICT-257438.
